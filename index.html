Bio

Beneath the flannels, hoodies, and mystique are three wildly educated and talented musicians steam rolling their dreams. Jeff, from Albuquerque, New Mexico, started playing guitar at age 12 and followed his artistic desires to California. Pete too picked up the guitar in his pre-teens and aggressivephly pursued his love of the instrument through the Music program at UC Irvine. Ashley, hails from the San Francisco Bay Area as a passionate music lover and singer.

Though their chemistry was not instantaneous, Pete and Jeff unknowingly shared an affinity for the same music, Asian women, and of course ... Coachella. Following a few encounters and shenanigans with mutual friends, they both attended the festival in 2012, where Pete Henry and Jeff Omidvaran officially decided the time had come to combine their passions, creativity, and form a band that would later be named Hiya! Ashley Nguyen, having collaborated with Pete in a prior ensemble, was then brought on board to sharpen the trio with a unique sensibility and vibrant vocal character.

Together they have synergized technology with acoustic instrumentals and a message for the Dream Generation about fearlessness, drive, and life without regret. This Urban Folk band is at the beginning of their journey, and they appear to have created a new, incredibly accurate and structured sound. They are progressive and willing to take on the world, forever learning, growing, and perfecting their craft. Stay posted for future performance dates and album releases.
